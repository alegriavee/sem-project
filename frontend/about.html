About BAWASA

The BAWASA (Barangay Waterworks and Sanitation Association) Water Monitoring Dashboard is a web-based system designed to monitor water consumption, generate billing insights, and provide transparency for residents and local officials in Barangay Quipot.

This system helps streamline water utility management by visualizing data on monthly usage, identifying top and bottom consumers, and supporting ISO 9001-aligned service standards.

Developers
  • Adame, Veejay A.
  • Malco, Lealyn R.
  • Mulleda, Sophia Bianca T.
Purpose

This project aims to improve operational efficiency, promote accountability, and support data-driven decision-making for better water resource management within the community.
